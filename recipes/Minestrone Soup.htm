Minestrone Soup

4 cups vegetable stock

2 14.5 oz cans stewed tomatoes, undrained

2 tablespoons Italian seasoning

1 15 oz can kidney beans

1 Large potato, cubed

3 cups fresh corn kernels

1 onion, chopped

1 large zucchini, sliced

2 stalks celery, chopped

1 cup uncooked orzo pasta

2 carrots, chopped

1 large head cabbage, ﬁnely chopped

Salt and pepper to taste

In a large soup pot combine the vegetable stock, the tomatoes, potato, onion, celery, carrot, cabbage and Italian seasoning.  Bring to a boil and reduce heat. Simmer for about 3 hours. Stir in the beans, com, zucchini and pasta; simmer for 10-15 more minutes until the vegetables are tender. Season with salt and pepper.
