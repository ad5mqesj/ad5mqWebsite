Chunky Creamy Chicken Soup

1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2/3 cup onion, finely chopped
2 medium carrots, chopped
2 celery ribs, chopped
1 cup frozen corn
2 cans cream of potato soup
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 teaspoon dill weed
1 cup frozen peas
1/2 cup half and half

In slow cooker put chicken breasts, onion, carrots, celery and corn. 
In a large bowl, whisk the soup, broth and dill until blended; add to slow cooker. 
Cover and cook on low 4 hours or until chicken and vegetables are tender. 
Pull apart chicken breasts. Stir in the peas and half and half. 
Cover and cook for 30 minutes longer or until heated through.
