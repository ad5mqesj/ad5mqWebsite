Hash Brown Casserole

Marion Murray, 1992

1 2 lb bag frozen country-style hash browns, thawed

1 cup onion, diced

1/2 cup butter or margarine, thinly sliced

1 8 oz container sour cream

1 can Cream of Chicken soup

2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded

1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Stir together well the onion, sour cream, cheese, butter, and soup. Add the hash browns and mix well.

Spread mixture into a casserole dish and top with additional cheese. Bake at 350° for 1 – 1 1/2 hours.
