Lemon Icebox Pie

1 9-inch graham cracker crust

2 8 oz packages cream cheese, softened

1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk

2 lemons, juiced

1 teaspoon lemon zest

In a medium mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add condensed milk, lemon juice, and lemon rind. Mix until smooth. Pour mixture into crust. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving. Garnish with Cool Whip and mint leaves, if desired.
