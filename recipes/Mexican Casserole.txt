Mexican Casserole
Carol Eschen, 2002

2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 can chopped green chiles
1 lb hamburger, browned
1 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon dry onion flakes
cheddar cheese, grated
tortilla chips

Mix together first 5 ingredients and spread over broken tortilla chips in a 9 x 13-inch greased pan. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 400° 20 - 30 minutes.
