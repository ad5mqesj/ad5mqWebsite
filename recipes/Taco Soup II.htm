Taco Soup

Cindy Bledsoe, 2004

2 lbs hamburger

1 onion, chopped

2 cans Ranch Style beans

2 cans Mexican Tomatoes

1 can Rotel tomatoes

1 can corn

1 package dry Hidden Valley Ranch dressing mix

1 package Taco seasoning

1/2 - 1 cup water depending on how soupy you want it to be

grated cheese

Fritos or tortilla chips

Brown the hamburger and onion, drain. Add the beans, corn, tomatoes, taco seasoning, Hidden Valley mix, Rotel, corn and water. Simmer for 20 minutes. To serve, put the chips in a bowl first followed by the soup, and finally the cheese.
