Pork Chops Dijon

4 pork chops

1 tablespoon oil

1 clove garlic

1 tablespoon butter, melted

1 - 1 1/2cups Italian bread crumbs

1 1/2 tablespoons parsley

1 egg, slightly beaten

1 Tablespoon water

1/2 cup milk

1 12 oz jar brown gravy

1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

In large skillet, brown chops on both sides in oil; remove and set aside. In small saucepan, combine butter, bread crumbs, parsley and pepper; set aside. Combine egg and water. Dip chops into egg mixture then crumb mixture. Place on well-greased baking sheet. Bake at 400° for 12 - 15 minutes or until

cooked. Meanwhile pour excess fat from skillet; add milk and heat, stirring up browned bits. Stir in gravy and mustard. Serve chops with gravy.
