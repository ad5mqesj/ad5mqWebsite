Creamy Mushroom Rice

1 can Creamy Chicken Mushroom Soup
1 soup can water
1 tablespoon butter
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups dry Minute Rice

Combine soup, water, butter and salt in a saucepan. Bring to a full boil. Stir in rice. Cover, remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with fork.
