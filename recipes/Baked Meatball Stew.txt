Baked Meatball Stew

1/2 bag Italian Style Meatballs
1/2 bag Roasted Vegetable Blend
12 oz jar beef gravy
14 1/2 oz can diced tomatoes, Italian style
4 1/2 oz can mushrooms, drained

Preheat oven to 425°. Place meatballs and vegetables in an ungreased 9 x 13-inch baking dish. Mix remaining ingredients together in a bowl and pour over meatball/vegetable mixture. Spread out evenly. Cover pan tightly with foil. Bake for 55-65 minutes.

Slow Cooker Method

Place all ingredients except vegetables in slow cooker on low heat. Stir well.
Cook 6-8 hours. Add vegetables during last hour.
