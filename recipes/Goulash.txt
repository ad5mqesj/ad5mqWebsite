Goulash

1 lb ground beef
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 1/2 cups water
1 15 oz cans tomato sauce
1 15 oz cans diced tomatoes
1 tablespoons Italian seasoning
1 bay leaf
1/2 tablespoon salt
1/8 tablespoon pepper
1 cup macaroni

In large pot cook ground beef over medium heat until browned, spoon off any extra grease. Add onion and garlic, saute until transparent. Add water, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, all seasoning and bay leaf. Allow to simmer for 15 - 20 minutes. Add macaroni and allow to simmer an additional 20 minutes or until tender. Remove bay leaf before serving
