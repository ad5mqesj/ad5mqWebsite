Five-Cheese Chicken Breasts

 

2 chicken breasts, about 1 %- lbs, skinned, boned, split

4 thin slices each Mozzarella and Swiss cheese

1/2 cup Ricotta cheese

1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

1 egg yolk

Flour

1 egg, well beaten

1/4 - 1/3 cup fine dry bread crumbs

1 tablespoon each oil and margarine

Cheddar Cheese Sauce

Place chicken halves between pieces of plastic wrap. Flatten with mallet to form thin cutlets. Place a slice each of Mozzarella and Swiss cheese on each cutlet. Mix Ricotta and Parmesan cheeses with egg yolk. Divide among cutlets, placing a spoonful at wide end of each cutlet. Roll up from wide end.

Roll each in flour, dip in egg and coat with crumbs. Secure with toothpicks. Brown chicken on both side in hot oil and margarine. Arrange in shallow 1 1/2-quart baking dish. Bake at 350° for 20 minutes. Transfer rolls to hot serving dish, removing toothpicks. Cover with hot cheese sauce.

Cheddar Cheese Sauce

2 tablespoons margarine

2 tablespoons flour

1 1/4 cups milk

1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Dash hot pepper sauce

2 tablespoons dry sherry

1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Melt margarine in saucepan. Stir in flour. Add milk. Cook, stirring, until thickened. Stir in Worcestershire sauce, hot pepper, sherry and Cheddar cheese. Add salt and pepper to taste.
