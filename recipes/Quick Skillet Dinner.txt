Quick Skillet Dinner

1 8 oz package Beef Rice-a-Roni
1 lb lean hamburger
2 3/4 cups hot water

Brown hamburger in large skillet; drain. Add rice; continue cooking until rice is lightly browned. Add water and seasoning packet; stir. Bring to a boil; cover. Reduce heat; simmer 15 - 20 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
