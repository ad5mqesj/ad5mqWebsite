Sweet Potato Pie

1 1/4 cups firmly packed brown sugar

1 1/2 cups sweet potatoes, mashed

3/4 teaspoon cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ginger

1/4 teaspoon cloves

1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

1 cup evaporated milk

3 eggs

1 prepared pie crust

Heat oven to 350°. Combine filling ingredients except evaporated milk and eggs. Beat at medium speed, scraping bowl often, until well mixed. Add milk and eggs and beat on low speed until well mixed. Pour filling into crust. Bake for 50 - 60 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
