Applesauce Bars

1 16 oz jar sweetened applesauce ???

2 cups flour

1 cup sugar

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon baking soda

1 teaspoon cinnamon

1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

1 egg

1/2 cup shortening

1/2 teaspoon vanilla

1 cup raisins

Icing

1 cup powdered sugar

1 tablespoon butter, softened

1 to 2 tablespoons hot water

Grease a 15 x 10 x 1-inch jelly roll pan. Combine flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, cinnamon and nutmeg in a large bowl. Beat in egg, shortening and vanilla with mixer on medium speed; just until mixture is well blended. Stir in raisins. Pour into prepared pan, spreading evenly. Bake for 20 minutes, or until top springs back when lightly pressed with fingertip. Remove to wire rack. For icing, combine sugar with butter in a small bowl. Stir in water until mixture is of glaze consistency. Spread over cake and cut into squares.
