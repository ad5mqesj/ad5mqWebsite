Church Lady Potatoes
Shirley Gilbertson, 2010

1 bag shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
3 teaspoons onion flakes
1 stick margarine, melted

Topping

2 cups cornflakes
3/4 stick margarine, melted

Mix together the first 6 ingredients. Put in a 9 x 13-inch pan. Mix the topping ingredients together and spread on top. Bake, uncovered, at 350° for 1 hour.

Adding chopped ham, bacon or sausage makes it more like a meal.
