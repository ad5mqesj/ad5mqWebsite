Prehistoric Pudding

1 16 oz package Oreos
2 cups cold milk
1 4 oz package chocolate instant pudding
1 8 oz container Cool Whip, thawed

Crush cookies in food processor. Pour milk into large bowl. Add pudding mix and beat with a wire whisk for 2 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes. Stir in Cool Whip and 1/2 of the crushed Oreos. Place 1 tablespoon crushed cookies in 8 7 oz clear plastic cups. Fill each cup about 3/4 full with pudding mixture. Top with remaining crushed cookies. Refrigerate 1 hour.
