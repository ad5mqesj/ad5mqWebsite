Wacky Cake

3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons cocoa
2/3 cup vegetable oil
3 tablespoons vinegar
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups cold water

In a 9 x 13-inch ungreased pan, put flour, sugar, baking soda, salt and cocoa. Stir until mixed. Make 3 wells in mixture. Put vegetable oil in one well, vinegar in the second well and vanilla in the last well. Pour water over all. Mix with spoon until well dissolve. Bake at 350 for 25 - 30 minutes.
