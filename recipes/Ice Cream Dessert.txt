Ice Cream Dessert 
Gloria McLeod, 1983

1 package vanilla wafers, crushed and divided
2 cups powdered sugar
2 squares chocolate
1/2 cup butter
3 eggs, separated
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 gal vanilla ice cream, thoroughly softened

Spread the vanilla wafers in the bottom of a 9 x 13-inch dish, reserving some for topping. 
Melt chocolate and butter. Mix in sugar, egg yolks, vanilla and salt; cool. Add stiffly beaten egg whites. Pour chocolate mixture over the vanilla wafers in dish and cover with ice cream. Sprinkle reserved vanilla wafers over the top. Cover and freeze.
