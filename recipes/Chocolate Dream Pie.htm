Chocolate Dream Pie

2 envelopes Dream Whip

2 3/4 cups milk

2 4 serving size packages instant chocolate pudding

9 in graham cracker pie crust

1/2 Teaspoon vanilla

Prepare topping with 1 cup of the milk and vanilla. Whip on high speed for 2 minutes until light and fluffy peaks form. Add remaining milk and the pudding. Blend; then beat at high speed for 2 minutes. Spoon into pie crust.
