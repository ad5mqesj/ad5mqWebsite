Cheesy Pizza Potatoes

1 lb ground beef

1 package au gratin potatoes

1 cup hot water

2/3 cup milk

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning

1/4 teaspoon pepper

1 16 oz can whole tomatoes, undrained

1 4 oz can mushroom stems and pieces, drained

1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese

Cook and stir beef over medium high heat until brown; drain. Mix beef and remaining ingredients except cheese in 2-quart casserole, break up tomatoes. Bake at 400° uncovered until potatoes are tender,

45 - 50 minutes; sprinkle with cheese. Bake until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes.
