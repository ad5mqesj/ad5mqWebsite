Carrot Bars

4 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
3 jars baby food carrots

Cream Cheese Icing

1 8oz package cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 tablespoon milk
1 teaspoon vanilla

Beat eggs until light. Add all other ingredients. Mix well. Pour into greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350ยบ for 30 - 40 minutes. Mix icing ingredients. Once bars are cool, frost with icing.
