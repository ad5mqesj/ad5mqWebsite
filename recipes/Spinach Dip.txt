Spinach Dip
Maxine Murray, 1990

1 box frozen chopped spinach
1 box Knorr dry vegetable soup mix
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 3 oz cream cheese
green onions, sliced

Cook spinach according to package. Drain well. Mix the next 4 ingredients before putting spinach in. Serve in bread ball.
