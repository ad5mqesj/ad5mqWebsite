Chilli Mac and Cheese

1 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
garlic
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 tablespoon chilli powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup kidney beans, drained
26 oz can diced tomatoes
4 cups chicken broth
10 oz elbow pasta
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, brown ground beef with onion, drain. Add garlic, cayenne pepper, chilli powder, salt and pepper; stir. Add kidney beans, tomatoes and chicken broth. Bring to a boil. Add pasta and cook until pasta is done. Stir in cheddar cheese.
