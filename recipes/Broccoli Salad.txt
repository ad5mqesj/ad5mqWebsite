Broccoli Salad

3 bunches broccoli, bite-size pieces
1/2 cup celery, chopped
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
8 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
1 1/2 cup mayonnaise
3/4 cup sugar
3 teaspoons apple cider vinegar

Mix mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar. Mix in broccoli, celery, raisins, sunflower seeds and bacon. Refrigerate overnight.
