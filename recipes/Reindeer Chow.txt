Reindeer Chow

1 14 oz box Chex Corn cereal
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/3 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups powdered sugar
2 cups Christmas M&MUs

Pour Chex cereal into a large bowl and set aside. In another bowl combine chocolate chips, peanut butter and butter. Melt in the microwave on high for 1 minute. Stir and continue heating for another 30 seconds. Remove from microwave. Add vanilla then mix well. Pour chocolate mixture over the cereal and stir to evenly distribute. Add powdered sugar, cover bowl and shake until evenly coated.
