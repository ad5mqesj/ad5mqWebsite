Burger Bundles

4 12 x 18 inch sheets aluminum foil
4 chopped beef burgers
1 bag country vegetable blend
4 onion slices

Preheat oven to 450°. Spray center of each sheet of foil with nonstick cooking spray. Center one burger on foil. Top burger with onion slice. Place approximately 1/4 bag of vegetables on and around burger. Season to taste. To form a bundle, bring sides together and double fold. Double fold ends. Do not flatten bundle; leave room for heat to circulate. Repeat to make four bundles. Bake 20 - 24 minutes on baking sheet in oven.
