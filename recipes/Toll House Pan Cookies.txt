Toll House Pan Cookies

2 1/4 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup margarine, softened
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
1 12 oz package semi-sweet chocolate chips

Preheat oven to 375°. In small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside. In large bowl, combine margarine, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla; beat until creamy. Beat in eggs. Gradually add flour mixture; mix well. Stir in chocolate chips. Spread into greased 10 x 15 x 1-inch baking pan. Bake for 20 minutes. Cool.
