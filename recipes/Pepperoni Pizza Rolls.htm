Pepperoni Pizza Rolls

1 tube refrigerator pizza crust dough (not thin crust)

1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

1/2 cup sliced pepperoni

1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

pizza sauce

Preheat oven to 425°. Using a rolling pin, roll tube of pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into a rectangle about 1/4 inch thick. Sprinkle with the garlic salt, Italian seasoning, pepperoni and cheeses. Starting at a short end, roll dough tightly to form a log. Slice into 1-inch pieces. Place rolls on lightly greased baking sheet. Bake for 12 - 15 minutes or until browned on top. Serve with pizza sauce.
