Monkey Bread
Gae Piotti, 2010

4 tubes refrigerator biscuits
1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 3/4 cups sugar, separated
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup evaporated milk
3/4 cup butter

Mix cinnamon with 3/4 cup sugar. Cut each biscuit into 4 pieces and roll in sugar cinnamon mix. Place them in a tube pan. Mix 1 cup sugar, brown sugar, evaporated milk and butter in saucepan; bring to boil and pour over the biscuits. Bake at 350° for 30 minutes or until it looks done.
