Country Meatball Ragout

1 1/2 lb ground beef
1/3 bread crumbs
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 can beef broth
1 8 oz can tomatoes, cut up
1/2 teaspoon basil
1/8 teaspoon thyme
1 small bay leaf
1 20 oz package frozen stew vegetables

Mix thoroughly beef, bread crumbs, egg, onion, salt and a dash of pepper; shape firmly into 24 meatballs. In large saucepan, brown meatballs in 2 tablespoons shortening; pour off fat. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to boil; reduce heat. Cover; simmer 20 minutes or until done. Stir occasionally.
Gradually blend 1/2 cup water into 1/4 cup flour until smooth; slowly stir into stew. Cook, stirring until thickened. Remove bay leaf.
