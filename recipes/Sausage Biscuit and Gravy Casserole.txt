Sausage, Biscuit and Gravy Casserole

1 tube buttermilk biscuits
1 lb breakfast sausage
8 eggs
1 cup milk
salt & pepper to taste
2 2.64 packages of country gravy mix

Heat oven to 375. 
Grease a 9 x 13-inch baking dish. 
Cut biscuits into 1-inch pieces and line bottom of baking dish. 
Brown sausage and drain. Layer cooked sausage over biscuit pieces. 
Mix eggs, milk, salt and pepper. 
Pour egg mixture over sausage and biscuits. 
Make gravy according to package directions. 
Pour over sausage, biscuits and eggs. 
Bake 1 hour. Allow to cool five minutes before serving.
