No Bake Cookies
Bonnie Herrington, 1972

2 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup cocoa
1/2 cup peanut butter
3 cups oatmeal
Dash salt

In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, butter, cocoa, and milk. Stirring constantly, bring to boil. Boil hard for 1 minute. Remove from heat. Add vanilla, peanut butter, and oatmeal. Spoon out blobs quickly onto wax paper.
