Cheesy Baked Green Beans

2 lbs green beans, cleaned with ends removed or 3 cans green beans
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1 cup mozzarella
2/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for garnish
salt
2 teaspoons lemon zest

Preheat oven to 400. Place green beans in a cookie sheet. Pour cream over beans and scatter garlic. Season with salt. Add lemon zest. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan and bake until beans are tender or heated through and cheese is melted, 25 Ð 30 minutes. If desired, broil until cheese is browned. Garnish with more Parmesan and serve.
