Oven-Baked Frito Pie

1 lb ground beef
1 16 oz can chili beans
1 8 oz can tomato sauce
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
3 1/2 cups Fritos
1 1/4 cup sour cream

Cook ground beef until meat is browned; drain. Stir in beans, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, taco seasoning and 1/4 cup of cheese. Sprinkle 1 cup Fritos in bottom of 8 x 8-inch baking dish. Cover with chili. Bake at 350ยบ for 20 minutes. Spread sour cream over chili. Top with remaining Fritos and cheese. Bake 4- 5 minutes longer.
