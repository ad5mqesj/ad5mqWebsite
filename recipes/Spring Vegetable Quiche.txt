Spring Vegetable Quiche

4 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 10 oz package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 package Knorr Spring Vegetable recipe mix
9-inch pie crust

Preheat oven and baking sheet to 350°. In large bowl, with wire whisk, beat eggs. Blend in milk, cheese, spinach and Knorr Spring Vegetable recipe mix. Pour into pie crust. Bake on baking sheet 50 minutes or until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean.
