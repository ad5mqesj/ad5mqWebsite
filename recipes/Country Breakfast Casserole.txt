Country Breakfast Casserole

1 large onion, chopped
3 cups frozen hash browns
1 lb pork sausage
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup milk
6 eggs, beaten

Heat oven to 400°. Grease 9 x 13-inch baking dish. Cook sausage, onion, and hash browns. Pour into baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. In medium bowl, combine pepper, milk and eggs; beat with wire whisk until smooth. Pour over sausage mixture. Bake for 35 - 40 minutes keeping covered until last 15
minutes.
