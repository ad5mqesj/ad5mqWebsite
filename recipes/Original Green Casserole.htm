Original Green Casserole

2 cans cut green beans

3/4 cup milk

1 can cream of mushroom soup

1/8 teaspoon pepper

1 can French fried onions

Combine beans, milk, soup, pepper and 1/2 can French fried onions; pour into 1 ½-quart casserole. Bake at 350° for 30 minutes. Top with remaining onions and bake 5 minutes longer.
