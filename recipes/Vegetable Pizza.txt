Vegetable Pizza

2 packages Crescent rolls
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 8 oz packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
1 envelope Ranch dressing mix

3/4 cup of each: 	
cauliflower, finely chopped
broccoli, finely chopped
carrots, shredded
lettuce (optional)
tomato, finely chopped
green pepper, finely chopped

Press Crescent roll dough flat on a cookie sheet. Prick with a fork. Bake as directed on package. Let cool. Combine cream cheese, mayonnaise and Ranch mix. Spread on baked Crescent roll. Layer with vegetables ending with cheddar cheese. Cut into squares and serve.
