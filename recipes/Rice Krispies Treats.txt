Rice Krispies Treats

1/4 cup margarine
1 10 oz package marshmallows
6 cups Rice Krispies cereal

Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat. Add cereal. Stir until well coated. Using buttered spatula, press mixture evenly into a 9 x 13-inch pan coated with cooking spray.
