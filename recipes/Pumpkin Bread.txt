Pumpkin Bread

2 cups sugar
2/3 cup oil
4 eggs
1 15 oz can pumpkin
1/2 cup water
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 Teaspoon nutmeg
2 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda

Beat together sugar and oil. Add eggs, pumpkin, water, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour and baking soda. Pour into loaf pans. Bake at 375° for 50 - 55 minutes. Bread is done when a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
