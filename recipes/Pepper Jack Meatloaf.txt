Pepper Jack Meatloaf

1 lb ground beef
1 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup onion, diced
1 cup oatmeal
2 eggs
2 cups shredded pepper jack cheese
1/4 cup ketchup
1/8 teaspoon chili powder
Salt and pepper

Preheat oven to 400. Mix ground beef, celery, onion, oatmeal, eggs, chili powder, salt and pepper together. Press half of the beef mixture into loaf pan. Place cheese over beef mixture. Top with remaining beef mixture and press down to seal. Spread ketchup over top of meatloaf. Cover lightly with foil, bake for 30 minutes.Increase oven temperature to 425, remove foil and continue to bake for 20 minutes or   until edges are bubbling and knife inserted in center comes out clean. Let sit for 5 minutes then slice and serve.
