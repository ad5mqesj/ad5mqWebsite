Seafood Chowder

3 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 cups milk
2 cans cream of potato soup
1 8 oz can creamed corn
1 8 oz package imitation crab
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 10 oz package frozen chopped broccoli

Sauté onions in butter. Add milk, soup corn, crab and seasonings. Heat slowly and stir frequently. Add broccoli. Simmer 15 minutes.
