Fruit Pizza

Michelle Killebrew, 2010

1 package refrigerated sugar cookie dough

1 8 oz package cream cheese, softened

1/2 cup powdered sugar

Assorted fruits (pineapple, strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, bananas)

Spread cookie dough in pizza pan. Bake for 8 minutes, just until the dough turns slightly brown. Let cool. Mix cream cheese and powdered sugar. Spread on cooled dough. Top with fruit. Double recipe and use a 10 x 15-inch baking pan when a bigger pizza is desired.
