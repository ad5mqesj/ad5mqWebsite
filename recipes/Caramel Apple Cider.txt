Caramel Apple Cider

4 cups apple cider
3 tablespoons caramel syrup
1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
Sweetened whipped cream

Pour cider into a 2-quart slow cooker. Put cinnamon and cloves in cheesecloth or a coffee filter, tie. 
Add bag to cider. Cook on low for 4 hours. Remove bag. Serve with whipped cream.
