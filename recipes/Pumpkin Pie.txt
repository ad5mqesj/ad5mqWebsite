Pumpkin Pie

1 1/2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon ginger
1/2 teaspoon cloves
4 eggs
1 29 oz can pumpkin
2 12 oz cans evaporated milk
2 unbaked 9 in pie shells

Mix sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger and cloves in small bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture. Gradually stir in evaporated milk. Pour into pie shells. Bake in preheated 425° oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350°; bake 40 - 50 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
