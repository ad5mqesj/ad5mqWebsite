No Bake Peanut Butter Pie

1 8 oz package cream cheese
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup milk
1 16 oz Cool Whip, thawed
2 9-inch graham cracker crusts

Beat together cream cheese and powdered sugar. Mix in peanut butter and milk. Beat until smooth. Fold in Cool Whip. Spoon into graham cracker crusts; cover, and freeze until firm.
