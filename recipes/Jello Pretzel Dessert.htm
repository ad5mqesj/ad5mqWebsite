Jello Pretzel Dessert

1 1/2 cups coarsely crushed pretzels

3 tablespoons sugar

3/4 cup margarine, melted

1 8 oz package cream cheese, softened

1 cup powdered sugar

1 8 oz container Cool Whip

1 6 oz package strawberry Jello

2 1/4 cups boiling water

2 10 oz packages frozen strawberries

For crust, stir together pretzels, 3 tablespoons sugar and butter. Press into 9 x 13-inch pan. Bake at 350° for 7 minutes. Let cool. For first layer, mix together well cream cheese, powdered sugar and Cool Whip. Spread over the cooled crust. For second layer, dissolve Jello in boiling water. Add strawberries. Refrigerate until syrupy. Pour over first layer. Refrigerate until well set.
