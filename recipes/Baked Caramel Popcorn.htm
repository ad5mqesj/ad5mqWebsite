Baked Caramel Popcorn

Shannon Marx, 2009

1 cup margarine

2 cps brown sugar

1/2 cup light corn syrup

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1 teaspoon vanilla

6 quarts popped corn (not microwavable)

Melt margarine. Stir in sugar, syrup, and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Then boil without stirring for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in baking soda and vanilla. Pour over popped corn and mix well. Put into a large, shallow pan. Bake at 250° for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes.
