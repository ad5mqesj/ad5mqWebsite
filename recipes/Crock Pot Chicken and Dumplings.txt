Crock Pot Chicken & Dumplings

4 - 6 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 tablespoons butter
2 cans cream of chicken soup
3 cans chicken broth
1 onion, diced
1 tablespoon dried parsley
4 Grands flaky refrigerator biscuits

Place chicken in crock pot. Add the cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, onions and parsley. Cook on high for 4 - 6 hours. After cooking time has elapsed, take a fork and break the chicken into pieces. Cut each biscuit into 10 pieces. Stir into hot chicken and continue to cook for 30 minutes. Add more chicken broth as necessary.
