Sweet Potato Casserole

2 40 oz cans sweet potatoes, drained
4 tablespoons butter
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
1/4 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup flour
1 cup dark brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup quick-cooking oats
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 stick butter
2 cups miniature marshmallows

Preheat the oven to 350. Spray a 9 x 13-inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Mash the sweet potatoes with the butter until mostly smooth. Add the eggs, salt, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, vanilla and nutmeg. Beat until you have a uniform mixture. Add 1/2 cup brown sugar and cream. Mix well. Pour into baking dish. Cover with marshmallows. In a separate bowl combine the flour, 1 cup brown sugar, salt, oats and 1 teaspoon cinnamon. Stir together well. Stir in stick of butter with a fork until you have a crumbly mixture. Crumble over the marshmallows. Bake 30 - 45 minutes.
