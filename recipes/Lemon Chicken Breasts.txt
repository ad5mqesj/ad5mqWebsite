Lemon Chicken Breasts

Boneless chicken strips
Flour for coating
Salt and Pepper
1 tablespoon oil
5 tablespoons margarine
2 tablespoons lemon juice
3 tablespoons chicken broth
3 tablespoons chopped parsley

Season flour with salt and pepper and then coat chicken. Heat oil and 3 tablespoons margarine in large skillet. Add chicken and sauté 5 - 6 minutes on each side. Place in dish and keep hot. Add lemon juice and broth to pan juices. Bring to boil. Boil 1 minute. Add parsley and remaining 2 tablespoons of margarine. Stir until blended. Pour over chicken.
