Pepperoni Rigatoni

8 oz rigatoni noodles, cooked

1 cup pepperoni, cubed

1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

1/2 cup diced onion

Basil

Garlic

1 26 oz jar spaghetti sauce

2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded

Combine all ingredients, reserving some mozzarella. Bake in a greased 9 x 13-inch dish at 375° for 25 minutes. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and bake 5 more minutes.
