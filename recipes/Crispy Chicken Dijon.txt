Crispy Chicken Dijon

1/2 cup Dijon mustard
8 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (about 2 lbs)
1 cup plain dry crumbs
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Spread mustard over chicken. Coat with bread crumbs. In large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons oil over medium heat. Add half of chicken and cook 4 - 5 minutes on each side or until browned and done. Remove from skillet and keep warm. Repeat with remaining chicken, adding remaining oil as needed.
