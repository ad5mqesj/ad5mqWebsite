Earth Bread

1 cup zucchini, grated

1/2 cup carrots, grated

1/2 cup bananas, mashed

3 eggs

1 cup oil

2 cups sugar

2 teaspoon vanilla

3 cups flour

1 teaspoon soda

1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

3/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

1/2 cup nuts, coarsely chopped (optional)

Mix together zucchini, carrots, bananas, eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla. Gradually add dry ingredients. Pour into well-greased loaf pans. Bake at 350° for 1 hour.

Can substitute canned pumpkin in place of zucchini and carrots for pumpkin bread.
