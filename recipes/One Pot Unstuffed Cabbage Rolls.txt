One Pot Unstuffed Cabbage Rolls

1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup onion, diced
1 small green cabbage head, chopped (about 4 cups)
2 14.5 oz cans diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 can beef broth
1 teaspoon paprika
1 tablespoon brown sugar
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
4 cups cooked rice

In a Dutch oven, brown ground beef and onions. Cook until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease. Add cabbage, tomatoes, tomato sauce, broth, paprika, brown sugar, salt, pepper and garlic powder and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat and simmer until cabbage is tender, about 45 minutes. Serve over rice.
