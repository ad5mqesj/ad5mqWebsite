Pasta Salad

Cindy Russell, 2009

1 16 oz package Ditalini pasta, cooked and drained

1 bunch celery, chopped

6 eggs, hard-boiled and chopped

1 bunch green onions, sliced

2 cups mayonnaise

1/4 cup mustard

2 teaspoons salt

1/2 teaspoon pepper

Mix pasta, celery, eggs, and onions. In separate bowl mix together, mayonnaise, mustard, salt and pepper. Pour mayonnaise mixture over pasta mixture and mix well. Refrigerate.
