Cabbage and Sausage Foil Packets

1 lb Polish sausage, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 head of cabbage, cut into 1/2-inch wedges
20 small potatoes, quartered
1 small onion, sliced
garlic butter
salt and pepper to taste
4 ice cubes

Preheat grill to 400 degrees. Tear off 4 18 x 12 inch sheets of foil. Spray well with non-stick spray. Divide the cabbage, potatoes and onion over the foil sheets. Add 1 ice cube to each mound. Evenly divide the sausage between the packets and top with garlic butter (1-2 tablespoons per packet), salt and pepper. Seal each packet well and place on grill. Grill for 65 minutes flipping from the packets every 15 minutes. Allow to rest 10 minutes before serving. Serve with sour cream if desired.
These can be baked in the oven at 425 degrees for 55 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
