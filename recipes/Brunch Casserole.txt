Brunch Casserole

1 cup shredded hash browns, thawed
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup bacon, fried and crumbled
1/2 cup chopped onion (optional)
5 eggs
1 cup milk

Mix hash browns and bacon, cheese and onion. Place in greased casserole dish. In separate bowl, mix milk and eggs very well. Pour over hash brown mixture. Bake at 350° for 1 1/2 hours or until top is firm to touch. Cover casserole for first hour of baking.
