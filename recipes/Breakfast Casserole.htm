Breakfast Casserole

5 eggs

1 cup milk

1 lb. bacon, cooked and drained*

1/2 Teaspoon salt

1/2 Teaspoon pepper

 shredded cheddar cheese

 pie crust

Beat eggs well. Add milk, salt and pepper. Mix well. Add bacon and cheese.

Pour into pie crust. Bake at 350° for 55 minutes.  Casserole is done when a knife inserted between the middle and edge comes out clean.

*1 lb. sausage can be substituted; omit salt
