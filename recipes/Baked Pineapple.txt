Baked Pineapple

5 slices white bread, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 20 oz can crushed pineapple in juice, drained
1/3 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
3 eggs

Preheat oven to 350. Grease a 2-quart casserole dish. In a mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar. 
Beat in the eggs, one at a time. Stir in the drained crushed pineapple. Fold in bread cubes. 
Pour mixture into baking dish. 
Bake until lightly browned, about 35 – 45 minutes or until a knife inserted comes out clean.
