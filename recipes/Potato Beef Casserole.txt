Potato Beef Casserole

1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 tablespoons chopped celery leaves
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon pepper
4 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1 teaspoon salt

In a skillet, cook beef, onion, celery and celery leaves over medium heat until meat is no longer pink and vegetables are tender; drain. Remove from the heat; stir in soup, milk, Worcestershire sauce and pepper. Place half of the potatoes in a greased 2-quart baking dish; sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Top with half of the beef mixture. Repeat layers. Cover and bake at 400 for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
