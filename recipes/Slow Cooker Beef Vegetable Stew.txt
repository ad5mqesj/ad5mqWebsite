Slow Cooker Beef Vegetable Stew

2 packages beef tips and gravy
1 bag country vegetable blend

Place beef tips and gravy in slow cooker. Top with country vegetable blend. Cook on high 2 hours. Reduce heat to medium and cook for additional 2 - 4 hours. (For unattended method, cook for 8 - 10 hours on low to medium heat.)
