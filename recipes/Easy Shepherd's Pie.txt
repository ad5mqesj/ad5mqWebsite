Easy Shepherd's Pie

1 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 15 oz can mixed vegetables or 2 cans other vegetables
1 can cream of mushroom or celery soup
Salt and pepper to taste
Mashed potatoes
Shredded cheddar cheese

Preheat oven to 350°. In a large skillet, cook ground beef until brown over medium-high heat. Drain fat. Stir in onions and let cook for a little while. Add vegetables, soup, salt and pepper, heat through. Pour into baking dish coated with cooking spray. Cover with mashed potatoes. Bake for 30 minutes or until potatoes are golden and beef mixture is hot and bubbly. Add cheese. Return to oven until cheese is melted.
