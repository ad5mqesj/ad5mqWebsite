Pizza Casserole

2 lbs ground beef or 1 lb ground beef and 1 lb Italian sausage
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 jar spaghetti sauce
2 4 1/2 oz cans sliced mushrooms, drained
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon oregano
Dash pepper
2/3 package wide egg noodles, cooked and drained
1 6 oz package pepperoni
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

In a large frying pan, brown beef and onion until meat is no longer pink; drain. Add spaghetti sauce, mushrooms, salt, garlic powder, oregano and pepper. Spoon 1/2 mixture into a slow cooker. Top with half of the noodles, pepperoni and cheeses. Repeat layers. Cover and cook on low for 3 hours.
