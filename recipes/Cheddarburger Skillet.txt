Cheddarburger Skillet

1 lb ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 16 oz cans whole potatoes, drained and sliced
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

In skillet, brown beef and cook onion until tender. Stir to separate meat; pour off fat. Stir in soup and milk; add potatoes and cheese. Heat; stir occasionally.
