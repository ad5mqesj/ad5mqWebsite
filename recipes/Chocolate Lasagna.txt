Chocolate Lasagna

1 package Oreo cookies
6 tablespoons butter, melted
8 oz package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons cold milk
12 oz container Cool Whip, divided
2 3.9 oz packages chocolate instant pudding
3 1/4 cups cold milk
1 1/2 cups mini chocolate chips

Crush Oreo cookies, pour into a 9 x 13-inch dish. Stir in butter and press into bottom of dish. Refrigerate. Mix the cream cheese, 2 tablespoons milk and sugar together. Stir in 1 1/4 cups Cool Whip. Spread over crust. In a bowl, combine chocolate instant pudding with 3 1/4 cups cold milk. Whisk for several minutes until the pudding starts to thicken. Spread the chocolate pudding mixture over the cream cheese layer. Allow the pudding to set up for 5 minutes. Spread the remaining Cool Whip over the top. Sprinkle the mini chocolate chips evenly over the top. Place in the refrigerator for 4 hours before serving.
