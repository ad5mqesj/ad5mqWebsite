Vegetable Beef Soup

3 packages beef cubes
8 teaspoons instant beef bouillon
4 1/2 teaspoons salt
3/4 teaspoon marjoram
3/4 teaspoon thyme
3/4 teaspoon pepper
3 bay leaves
water
onion
2 cans tomato juice
16 oz package frozen green beans
16 oz package frozen peas
16 oz package frozen corn
16 oz package frozen carrots
5 cups uncooked elbow macaroni

Cook beef in slow cooker until done. Pour beef and juice into pot. Add spices water, onion, tomato juice and vegetables. Bring to boil and simmer until vegetables are near done. Add macaroni. Simmer until macaroni is done.
