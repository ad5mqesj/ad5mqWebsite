Stuffed Mushrooms
Susan Leeper, 2011

1 lb sausage, regular or spicy
Mushrooms, 2 small or 1 large, cleaned
1 8 oz package cream cheese

Brown sausage, drain. Mix together with cream cheese. Remove stems from mushrooms. Fill mushroom caps with sausage mixture. Bake at 350° for 20 - 25 minutes.
