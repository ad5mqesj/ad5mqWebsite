Chicken Mozzarella

4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

1 can tomato soup

1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

4 cups hot cooked corkscrew macaroni, cooked

Place chicken in 2-quart shallow baking dish. Mix soup, Italian seasoning and garlic powder. Spoon over chicken and bake at 400° for 20 minutes until chicken is done. Sprinkle cheese over chicken. Remove chicken. Stir sauce. Serve with macaroni.
