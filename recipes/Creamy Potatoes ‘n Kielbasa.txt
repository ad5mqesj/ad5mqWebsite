Creamy Potatoes & Kielbasa

3 cups O'Brian hash brown potatoes
1 lb kielbasa or Polish sausage, cut into 1/4-inch slices
1 can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup water
Salt and pepper

In a slow cooker, combine all ingredients. 
Cover and cook on low for 6 - 8 hours or until potatoes are tender.
