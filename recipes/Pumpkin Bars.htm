Pumpkin Bars

1 15 oz can pumpkin

3 eggs

1/3 cup sugar

1/3 cup vegetable oil

1 package yellow cake mix

1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice

1 16 oz can vanilla frosting

1 3 oz package cream cheese, softened

1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

In a large mixing bowl, combine the pumpkin, eggs, sugar and oil; mix well. Add cake mix and pumpkin pie spice; beat for 2 minutes. Pour into a greased 10 x 15 x 1-inch baking sheet. Bake at 350° for 25 - 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. In a mixing bowl, combine the frosting, cream cheese and cinnamon. Spread over cake. Store in refrigerator.
