Chicken Salad Sandwiches

1/4 cup mayonnaise

1/4 cup sour cream

2 teaspoons lemon juice

1 teaspoon Dijon-style mustard

3 teaspoons horseradish

2 tablespoons parsley

1/2 cup green onions, sliced

1/2 cup celery, chopped

1 12.5 oz can white meat chicken, broken up

lettuce

sandwich rolls

Mix mayonnaise, sour cream, lemon juice, mustard, horseradish and parsley. Add green onions, celery and chicken. Make sandwiches using lettuce and rolls.
