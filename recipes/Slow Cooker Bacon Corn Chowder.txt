Slow-Cooker Bacon Corn Chowder

1 lb small red potatoes, cut into 1-inch cubes
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 12 oz bags frozen whole kernel corn
3 cups chicken broth
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 cups half and half
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 lb bacon, cooked and crumbled

In 3 1/2 Ð 4 quart slow cooker, mix potatoes, onion, corn, broth, salt and pepper. Cover; cook on high 3 Ð 4 hours or until potatoes are tender. In small bowl, beat half and half and cornstarch with whisk until smooth. Stir half and half mixture and bacon into corn mixture. Cover; cook 10 - 15 minutes longer or until slightly thickened.
