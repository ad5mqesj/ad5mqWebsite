Chicken Enchiladas

Maxine Murray, 1998

2 cups cubed chicken

Onion, chopped

4 oz can green chiles

Few drops Tabasco

8 oz package cream cheese

8 oz container sour cream

1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Salt to taste

8 in flour tortillas

Whipping cream

Cheddar cheese, shredded

Salsa

Mix chicken, onion, chiles, Tabasco, cream cheese, sour cream, cayenne pepper and salt. Roll mixture in tortillas. Pour whipping cream over and add cheddar cheese. Bake at 350° for 30 minutes.
